1. Getting Started

Welcome to ByteWise Nutritionist!

ByteWise Nutritionist is a comprehensive Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help you track your nutrition, monitor your caloric intake, and achieve your health goals. The app uses authentic USDA food database information to provide accurate nutritional data.

System Requirements

Web Browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions recommended)
Internet Connection: Required for initial setup and food database searches
Device: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
PWA Installation: You can install ByteWise Nutritionist as an app on your device for offline access and a native app experience.

4. Food Tracking & Logging

[Screenshot: Food Search Interface]

Searching for Foods

1 Access Food Search: Click the search bar or "Add Food" button
2 Type Food Name: Enter the name of the food you want to log (e.g., "chocolate candy", "apple", "chicken breast")
3 Browse Results: The app will show you options from the USDA food database

Enhanced Candy Nutrition Data

For candy items, you'll see enhanced nutrition information that combines USDA data with detailed FoodStruct.com data, including comprehensive mineral content like calcium, iron, magnesium, and sodium.

Logging Meals

1 Select Food Item: Click on the food from search results
2 Choose Serving Size: Select or enter the appropriate serving size
3 Confirm Meal Type: The app will automatically detect meal type based on time:
  • Breakfast: 6:00 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Lunch: 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Dinner: 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Snack: 10:00 PM - 6:00 AM
4 Add to Log: Click "Add" to save the meal to your daily log
The app provides accurate calorie calculations using the standard 4-4-9 method (4 calories per gram of protein/carbs, 9 calories per gram of fat).

5. Calorie Calculator

[Screenshot: Calorie Calculator Interface]

Using the Calorie Calculator

1 Access Calculator: Find the calorie calculator in the main interface
2 Enter Ingredients: Type in multiple ingredients separated by commas
3 Specify Measurements: Include quantities (e.g., "1 cup rice, 2 tablespoons oil")
4 Calculate: Click "Calculate" to get comprehensive nutrition information

Understanding Results

The calculator provides:

  • Total Calories: Sum of all ingredients
  • Macronutrients: Protein, carbohydrates, and fat breakdown
  • Micronutrients: Vitamins and minerals when available
  • Per-ingredient breakdown: Individual nutrition for each item

11. PDF Export

Generating Nutrition Reports

PDF Report Contents

Your nutrition report includes:

  • Summary Statistics: Total calories, average daily intake

PDF reports are perfect for sharing with healthcare providers, nutritionists, or for keeping personal records of your nutrition journey.
